# Hikāya — Legal Essentials (templates)

Draft Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and Refund Policy so Hikāya can legally take payments and be compliant with the main data-protection regimes (GDPR/UK-GDPR and similar). Also included: a cookie/consent note and content-rights terms for uploaded material.

> **⚠️ Not legal advice.** I'm not a lawyer, and these are starting-point templates, not a substitute for review by a qualified lawyer in your jurisdiction (you're operating from Libya, selling globally, and processing EU personal data — that combination has real cross-border implications). Before you publish these: replace every `{PLACEHOLDER}`, confirm your legal entity and governing-law choice, and have a professional review the final text — especially the tax, consumer-refund (EU 14-day withdrawal right), and data-transfer sections. Payment processors (Stripe/Paddle) and app stores often *require* accurate Terms, Privacy, and Refund pages before they'll let you charge.

## 2. Privacy Policy

Last updated: {EFFECTIVE_DATE}

**1. Controller.** {COMPANY_LEGAL_NAME}, {COUNTRY_OF_ESTABLISHMENT}, {CONTACT_EMAIL}, is the data controller for personal data processed through {DOMAIN}.

- To send newsletters/marketing — *consent* (you can withdraw anytime).
- To measure and improve the product — *legitimate interests* (and *consent* for non-essential cookies where required).

## 3. Refund Policy

**Hikāya — Refund & Cancellation Policy**
Last updated: {EFFECTIVE_DATE}

**Subscriptions.** You can cancel anytime; cancellation stops future renewals and you keep access until the end of the current period. We don't generally refund partial periods, except where required by law.

**Free trials.** Cancel before the trial ends and you won't be charged. If you forget and are charged, contact us within {REFUND_WINDOW=14} days and we'll consider a refund in good faith.

**One-time purchases (story unlocks, credits).** Because digital content is delivered immediately, these are generally non-refundable once accessed. If a purchase was made in error or content is faulty/unavailable, contact us within {REFUND_WINDOW=14} days.

**EU/UK consumers — right of withdrawal.** You normally have 14 days to withdraw from a digital purchase. By starting to stream/download content immediately you may be asked to acknowledge that you lose this right for that item once delivery begins; where the right still applies, we honour it.

**How to request.** Email {CONTACT_EMAIL} from your account address with the purchase details. Approved refunds go back to the original payment method.

## 4. Cookie note (short banner + page)

**Banner:** "We use essential cookies to run Hikāya, and — with your consent — analytics cookies to improve it. [Accept] [Reject non-essential] [Manage]."

Implement consent before loading PostHog in regions that require it (EU/UK): only call `initAnalytics()` after consent, or use PostHog's consent/opt-out API. Essential/session cookies for sign-in don't require consent.

## 5. Content-rights terms (for uploads / marketplace)

Add this to the admin upload flow and, later, the author agreement. Anyone providing a story must confirm:

> "I own or have all rights necessary to upload this work and to have it converted into audio and distributed on Hikāya, including any translation and text-to-speech narration. I grant Hikāya a non-exclusive, worldwide licence to host, reproduce, narrate, and distribute it on the service. I will not upload public-domain claims that are false, or third-party works I don't have rights to."

This matters specifically because Hikāya ingests PDFs and generates voiced audio — verifying rights at the door protects you from infringement claims as the catalog grows.
